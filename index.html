Biography

I am an assistant professor at the Department of Applied Mathematics, National Sun Yat-sen University. Have received Ph.D. degree from National Chiao Tung University in Sep. 2017. I was a Postdoctoral research fellow from 2017 to 2021 at Institute of Statistical Science, Academia Sinica. I am interested in the area of data analysis and data security.

Interests

  • Statistical learning
  • Data analysis with CryoEM and medical image data
  • Dimension reduction and clustering analysis
  • Security system design and Side-channel analysis
